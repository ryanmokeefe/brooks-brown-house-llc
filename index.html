The Problem

Black Americans have an annual spending power of $1.2 Trillion, their households only hold a median of $11,000 wealth in 2013 "SOURCE - federal data"

What's even more disturbing is that by 2053 the median wealth for black families will fall to zero if the gap continues to widen at its current pace.

Median wealth for white Americans is 12 times higher than that of black Americans at around $140,000, and the inequality in wealth continues to grow and widen with each generation.

The Solution

Take Action!

Give Families the tools to become self sufficient over time, while learning about the homeownership process.

" Overall, housing equity makes up about two-thirds of all wealth for the typical (median) household. In short, for median families, the racial wealth gap is primarily a housing wealth gap." - Janelle Jones | February 13, 2017 | Ecomnomc Policy Institute

How it Works

Step 1

Supporter sits down with Brooks Brown House consultant to find the right price, square footage, and locations for porperty.

Step 2

Suporter waits 30 days to 3 months for Brooks Brown House complete investment property and return.

Step 3

Brooks Brown House will qualify eligible families for the Tenant to Homebuyer program, after apporval the family will sign to be placed in Supporters Property.

Step 4

After Tenant completes one year and is in good standing with the Landlord, tenants will qualify for BBH Tenant to Homebuyer program. BBH will replace your tenant when they transition. Experience less turnover risk with our program!

Tenant to Homebuyer Program
Tenant To Homebuyer Program Tenants winSupporters win
  1. Tenant signs a two year lease with landlord
  2. Lease term is completed and tenant is in good standing with the landlord
  3. Tenant sits down with BBH consultant to be introduced to credit advisor
  4. After credit advisor, tenant will meet with lender to get qualified for house
  5. After completion of qualification, tenant will sit with realtor to go over floor plan
  6. Tenant waits 1-4 months for house to be built to specifications
  7. Tenant sits with New York Life Insurance to pick a "Wealth Building Plan" for their family
  8. Tenant moves in!
  9. To stay in the loop, Homeowners will be connected on BBH Facebook group which will offer even more resources and videos. This will help guide what actions to take after becoming a homeowner!
